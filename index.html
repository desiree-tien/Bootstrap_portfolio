About Me

Originally from Colombia, Carlos came to the United States to complete a B.A. in Psychology from St. Edward's University in Austin, TX. During his time in college, he was actively involved in college live, academics, and sport. He was a coach for other students for over two years, played with the university's ultimate frisbee team, and complete two independent research studies looking at interactions between human behavior and technology. His interest in technology led him to enrolled in a full time coding bootcamp with The University of Texas at Austin, where he is currently gathering the skills to become a full stack developer.
